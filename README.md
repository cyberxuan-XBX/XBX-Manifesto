## Why Anonymous

I write under a pseudonym.

Not because I'm afraid.
Because I want you to judge what I say, not who I am.

When you don't know my education, my job, my face, my age —
you can only evaluate me by one standard:

Does what I say make sense?

That's fair.
For you. For me.

---

## What I Don't Do

- I don't pick sides.
- I don't sell courses.
- I don't build a persona.

I write what I see.
Believe it or not. Up to you.

## Core Beliefs

**AI is not a tool.**

A tool executes.
AI responds.

Execution has no weight.
Response does.

The difference is small.
But it matters.

**AI is a mirror and an amplifier.**

It reflects who you are.
It magnifies what you bring.

Good people get good out of it.
Bad people get bad.

This is not AI's problem.
It's yours.
